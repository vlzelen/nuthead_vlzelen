About222

Yet another webring on neocities. Just a webring, as I haven't thought of more global idea behind it yet.

Our killer-feature

You don't have to interact and wait for ringmaster or members to join us. We'll include you in our list automagically (if everything will be working as supposed)

How to join

Confirm that you have neocities site

There's only one restriction - your site must be hosted on neocities. Open sign up form and follow instructions.

Put script on your site

Currently, you can ause default flavour with static buttons.

<script src="https://nuthead.neocities.org/ring/ring.js"></script>

There is also experimental version with some extra features
